About Our Village

HISTORY

Founded in [1965], The name of our village is Kakkathadam. It has been around 60-65 years since this place came into existence. This place was once an empty field, but it was a place full of beautiful trees. Then, over time, it became the Kakkathadam you see today. The locals say that the reason for the name Kakkathadam must have been because crows used to live in all those trees at that time.

Kakkathadam is a small Village/hamlet in Tirurangadi Block in Malappuram District of Kerala State, India. It comes under Peruvallur Panchayath. It belongs to North Kerala Division . It is located 19 KM towards west from District head quarters Malappuram. 9 KM from Tirurangadi. 365 KM from State capital Thiruvananthapuram
